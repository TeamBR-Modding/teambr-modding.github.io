Welcome To Team BR Moding

Written by pauljoda on August, 22nd, 2015

The Team BR Modding organization is a sub group of the Beyond Reality Team. The members of this organization are ones from the Beyond Reality team and are more or less the technical members of the team. Primarily focused on mods, this group creates content relevant to the Minecraft community. Started by pauljoda and Dyonovan, the group has since moved on to create some very cool mods. Some of these mods include Neotech, Team BR Core (responsible for making some of the amazing things in the Beyond Reality Modpack possible), and more to come. They also work on mods imported to the group such as Modular Systems and Mob Tools.

This site serves as a central hub for all their work. Here you will find documentation on their mods as well as other useful information.
